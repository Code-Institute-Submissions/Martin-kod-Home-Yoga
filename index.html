Home Sweet Yoga

Advantages of Home Sweet Yoga practice


  • You will save time from going back and forth to a Yog Studio while still recieving feedback from a certified instructor.
  • Regular yoga practice makes you stronger, more flexible and more balanced, both physically and in your everyday life.
  • Whether you have never tried yoga before or you are a regular practitioner of yoga, Home Sweet Yoga delivers well thought-out sessions on three different levels: Beginner; Intermediate and Advanced.
Sign up now!

Beginner level

Our beginner class fits you who have never or seldom tried yoga before and feel like you want to work on your flexibility and balance while simultaneously building strength in your core. The beginner class include such yoga poses as child's pose, downward-facing dog and cobra pose, as well as the first warrior poses.

Advanced level

The advanced class are for experienced practitioners who have a well-rounded physique and can handle the most difficult poses in high tempo and in extended time periods. Our advanced class include working on such poses as crow pose, lotus, half monkey pose and chin stance.

Intermediate level

Our intermediate class is a great choice for you who have already practiced yoga for some time. Your flexibility, balance and strength are engaged during your practice and you are familiar with basic breath techniques and flows. Intermediate poses include dancer, standing forward split and half-moon pose.
